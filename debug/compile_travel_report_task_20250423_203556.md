### 2.2. Local Public Transit in Paris

*   **Available Types:** Metro (subway), RER (regional express network), bus, tram, Vélib' (bike-sharing).
*   **Key Routes for Tourists:**
    *   **Metro Line 1:** Louvre Museum, Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe.
    *   **Metro Line 4:** Notre Dame Cathedral, Montmartre.
    *   **Metro Line 6:** Eiffel Tower.
    *   **RER C:** Versailles Palace.
*   **Transit Pass Options:**
    *   **Navigo Easy:** Rechargeable card for single tickets (t+ tickets).
    *   **Navigo Découverte:** Weekly pass (valid Monday-Sunday). Requires a passport-sized photo. Most cost-effective for a week-long stay.
    *   **Paris Visite Pass:** Unlimited travel for 1, 2, 3, or 5 days.
*   **Approximate Costs:**
    *   Single t+ ticket: ~€2.10
    *   Navigo Easy (10 tickets): ~€16.90
    *   Navigo Découverte (weekly): ~€30.00
*   **Operating Hours:**
    *   Metro: 5:30 AM to 12:30 AM (later on weekends).
    *   Bus: 6:00 AM to 12:30 AM.
    *   RER: Similar to Metro hours.
* Frequency:
    * Metro: Every 2-8 minutes.
    * Bus: Every 5-15 minutes.
*   **Recommendation:** Purchase a Navigo Découverte pass if using public transport frequently. Validate your ticket before entering the platform.

### 2.3. Regional Transportation

*   **Train (SNCF):**
    *   **Routes:** High-speed TGV trains to other French and European cities (Lyon, Marseille, London, Brussels).
    *   **Service Providers:** SNCF.
    *   **Schedule Information:** SNCF Connect website or app. Booking in advance is recommended.
*   **Bus (Flixbus, BlaBlaBus):**
    *   **Routes:** Affordable connections to various destinations in France and Europe.
    *   **Schedule Information:** Flixbus or BlaBlaBus websites/apps. Slower than train travel.
