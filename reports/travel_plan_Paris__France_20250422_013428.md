## Local Guide

### 1. Brief History & Fun Facts:

Paris boasts a rich history, with the earliest settlements dating back to 8000 BC. The Parisii tribe established themselves on the Seine around 250-225 BC, building bridges and a fort. Conquered by the Romans in 52 BC, the city became Lutetia. In 508 AD, Clovis I, King of the Franks, made Paris his capital. During the Middle Ages, it flourished as a major European center for commerce, religion, and Gothic architecture.

Known as the "City of Light" since the 19th century due to its pivotal role in arts, sciences, and early adoption of street lighting, Paris is now the capital and most populous city in France. The city is home to world-renowned museums, architectural landmarks, and is a global hub for finance, fashion, and gastronomy.
