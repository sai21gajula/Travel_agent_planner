## 4. Local Guide: Le Marais (4th Arrondissement)**

Le Marais is a historic and vibrant district in Paris, known for its charming streets, boutiques, art galleries, and diverse culinary scene.

### 4.1. Top Attractions in Le Marais:

*   **Hôtel de Ville:** Place de l'Hôtel de Ville, 75004 Paris, France. A stunning city hall with historical significance and beautiful architecture.
*   **Artwork in City Hall Plaza:** Place de l'Hôtel de Ville, 75004 Paris, France.  Look for "La Science" and "L'Art" in the City Hall Plaza.
*   **Fondation Azzedine Alaia:** 18 Rue de la Verrerie, 75004 Paris, France. A museum dedicated to the work of fashion designer Azzedine Alaia.
*   **Mémorial de la Shoah:** Allée des Justes parmi les Nations, 75004 Paris, France. A Holocaust memorial museum.
*   **Musée National d'Art Moderne:** 19 Rue Beaubourg, 75004 Paris, France. The National Museum of Modern Art, housed in the Centre Pompidou (bordering Le Marais).
