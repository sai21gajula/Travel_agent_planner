#### 1. Brief History & Fun Facts

Paris boasts a rich history dating back to 8000 BC, with the Parisii tribe settling on the Seine River around 250-225 BC. The Romans conquered the area in 52 BC, establishing Lutetia. Clovis I, King of the Franks, made Paris his capital in 508 AD. Throughout the Middle Ages, Paris was Europe's largest city and a center for commerce, religion, and Gothic architecture. The University of Paris was established in the 13th century.

Fun Fact: Paris is known as the "City of Light" due to its early adoption of street lighting and its central role in the arts and sciences during the Enlightenment. As of 2023, Paris has an estimated population of over 2 million residents. The Louvre Museum was the most visited art museum in the world in 2022.
